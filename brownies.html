Cheesecake Brownies

Jazz up your standard boxed brownie mix with this recipe.

Ingredients

Brownie Mix:

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package brownie mix
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs

Cheesecake Topping:

  • 1 (8oz) package creem cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x13 inch pan
  2. Prepare brownie mix. Stir brownie mix, water, oil, and eggs in a medium bowl until well blended. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  3. Prepare cheesecake topping:Beat together cream cheese, egg, and sugar using an electric mixer until smooth. Dollop the cream cheese mixture on top of the brownie batter. Swirl together using a knife or skewer.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 22 to 25 minutes. Brownies will be done when a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool in the pan, then cut into squares and serve.
